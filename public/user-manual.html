Phone, tablet, or computer. Your data is saved online — clearing your browser won't delete anything.

Signing In

  1. 1
    Go to the app link above

    You'll see the sign-in screen with a car icon at the top

  1. 1
    Click "Register New User"

    It's a link below the Sign In button

  2. 2

  1. 1

Adding a Trip

Log every drive you take for work

💡 Miles and drive time fill in automatically once you enter both addresses — no calculator needed!
  1. 1
    Pick a Frequent Location (optional shortcut)

    Click the Frequent Locations dropdown at the top. Pick a job site you've been to before. It fills in the To Address and Location Name automatically. Don't see it? Pick ➕ Add a Location... to save it for next time.

  2. 2
    Fill in the Addresses

    From Address — where you started from.
    To Address — pick from the Frequent Locations drop down.

    💡 Click inside From or To first, then pick from Frequent Locations to fill that specific box.

  3. 3
    Miles fill in automatically

    After you tab out of the address fields, the miles and drive time calculate by themselves using online maps. You can type a different number if it's wrong.

  4. 4
    Pick or type a Purpose

    Use the Saved Purposes dropdown to pick a common reason (like "Site inspection"), or type your own. Pick ➕ Add a Purpose... to save a new one.

  5. 5
    Add a Description (optional)

    Any extra notes about the trip. Click the 🎤 microphone button to speak instead of type.

  6. 6
    Fill in Trip Details

    Date — auto filled when you added the trip
    Miles — auto filled from maps
    Drive Time (min) — auto-filled from maps
    Time on Site (min) — manually add — how long you spent at the location

  7. 7
    Click + Add Trip

    Your trip is saved and appears in the Trip Log below!

The Trip Log

View, edit, and manage all your recorded trips

The Trip Log is the list of all your trips at the bottom of the Trips tab. It shows a live summary bar with your totals.

Feature How to use it
Stats Bar The blue bar at the top shows your total trips, miles, reimbursement amount, drive minutes, and on-site minutes for the selected month
Filter by Month Use the month picker (top right of Trip Log) to see only one month's trips. Click All to see everything.
✏ Edit Click the Edit button on any trip to fix a mistake — change the date, address, miles, or any other field
✕ Delete Click the X button to permanently delete a trip. You'll be asked to confirm first.

Adding Expenses

Track money you spent for work

Use this tab for things like lunch with a client, office supplies, or any other work expense you want to get paid back for.

  1. 1
    Click the Expenses tab

    at the top of the app

  2. 2
    Fill in the form

    Date — when you spent the money
    Description — what you bought (e.g. "Lunch with adjuster")
    Amount — how much it cost in dollars

  3. 3
    Upload a scanned receipt or a picture

    Take a photo of your receipt with your phone and upload it. It's stored safely in the cloud. Click the receipt image later to view it.

  4. 4
    Click + Add Expense

    It appears in your expense list below

Running Reports

Print your monthly report to get reimbursed

📋

Mileage & Expense Report (IRS)

Submit this to Accounts Payable. Shows every trip, all expenses, and your total reimbursement amount.

🏠

Job Supervision Time Report

Shows how much drive time and on-site time you spent at each location. Sort by a single location to print just that one.

How to Print a Report

  1. 1
    Click the Report tab

    at the top of the app

  2. 2
    Pick the Report Type

    Choose Mileage & Expense (IRS) or Job Supervision Time

  3. 3
    Pick the Month
  4. 4
    (Supervision only) Filter by Location

    Pick one specific location to print just that one, or leave it on "All Locations"

  5. 5
    Click ↻ Generate

    Your report preview appears below

  6. 6
    Click 🖨 Print or 📊 Export Excel

    Print sends it to your printer. Export Excel saves it as a spreadsheet file.

Settings

Set up your info and shared team lists

📌 Frequent Locations Shared with your whole team. Save job site names and addresses here so everyone can pick them quickly. Add: type a Job Name + Address → click + Add Site. Delete: click the 🗑 button.
🗂 Saved Purposes Shared with your whole team. Save common trip reasons like "Site inspection" or "Electrical walkthrough" so everyone can pick them from a dropdown.
🏠 Home Address Add your home address in the Frequent Locations along with your name so it appears in the dropdown.
💰 Mileage Rate The dollar amount paid per mile. Default is $0.70 (IRS 2025 standard rate). Your admin may set a different rate.
🏢 Company Name Shows at the top of your printed reports.
📬 Submit To The department you send reports to (e.g. "Accounts Payable"). Shows on the report header.
⚠️ Always click ✓ Save Settings after making changes, or they won't be saved!

Tips & Tricks

Get the most out of the app

What you want to do How to do it
🎤 Speak instead of type Click the microphone 🎤 button next to the Description field and talk
➕ Add a new location while adding a trip Pick ➕ Add a Location... from the Frequent Locations dropdown — it takes you to Settings automatically
➕ Add a new purpose while adding a trip Pick ➕ Add a Purpose... from the Saved Purposes dropdown
📱 Use it like an app on your phone Open the link in your browser → tap Share → tap "Add to Home Screen" — it appears as an icon on your phone
✏️ Fix a mistake in a trip Click ✏ Edit on any trip in the Trip Log
📊 Export to Excel Go to Report tab → Generate → click Export Excel
🔄 Switch devices Just sign in on any device — all your data is online and synced automatically
📍 Fill From or To from saved locations Click inside the From or To field first, then pick from the Frequent Locations dropdown
🔒 My data is private Your trips and expenses are only visible to you. Locations and purposes are shared with the whole team.
